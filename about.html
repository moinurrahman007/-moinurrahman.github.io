About Me

I am a Business Systems & Operations Analyst with a foundation in Electronics & Communications Engineering. I enjoy standing between business and technology, turning requirements into structured flows, clear documentation, and stable systems that can scale across multiple countries.

What I Do in Practice

In my recent roles, I have worked at the intersection of fintech operations, digital field applications, and engineering teams. A few examples of how I create value:

  • Translating business requirements into clear tickets, flows, and documentation for development teams.
  • Supporting sbiCloud ERP, T24 CBS, and DFA systems used across multiple countries, coordinating with L2/DevOps when needed.
  • Using SQL, JQL, and tools like Postman to validate assumptions, test APIs, and confirm that changes behave as expected.
  • Helping structure SDLC and support processes so that issues are triaged, tracked, and resolved in a predictable way.
  • Communicating with non-technical stakeholders in simple language while keeping enough technical depth for engineers.

How I Like to Work

I’m motivated by environments where business teams, product managers, and engineers work closely together. I like to bring structure, clarity, and a bit of creativity into that mix.

  • Designing and improving business processes for digital products and financial services.
  • Working with APIs, logs, and data to understand what is really happening in production.
  • Using diagrams, flows, and checklists to align teams on how a system should behave.
  • Exploring AI-driven automation for operations and support workflows.
  • Doing creative work in photography, media, and storytelling, which helps me think visually about technical problems.
